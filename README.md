## Tactical Overview

I am a Mechatronics Engineer working at the intersection of **robotics, control systems, and machine learning**.

My research focuses on designing **intelligent autonomous systems capable of modeling, learning, and interacting with complex environments**.

**Research Interests**

• Reinforcement learning for embodied robotic systems  
• Learning-based control for autonomous vehicles  
• Hybrid physics-informed machine learning  
• Model predictive control for cyber-physical systems  
• Intelligent transportation systems

## Selected Research Work

### Residual Physics Learning for Nonlinear System Identification

Hybrid physics-informed neural network models combining analytical dynamics with learned residual models to improve nonlinear system prediction accuracy.

---

### Fuzzy Logic Control for Autonomous Vehicles

Design and simulation of a fuzzy logic steering and speed controller for autonomous vehicle trajectory tracking.

---

### Gen-HRES: Microgrid Resilience under Extreme Weather

Development of a conditional GAN framework to generate synthetic extreme weather scenarios used to evaluate MPC-based energy management strategies.
